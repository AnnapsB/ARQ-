How It Works

Your Path to Inner Balance

A simple, guided journey from discovery to lasting well-being.

1

Subscribe & Receive Your Kit

Choose your subscription plan and receive your beautifully packaged Arq wellness kit at home. Inside you'll find everything needed for your at-home blood sample collection, with clear, step-by-step instructions.

2

Complete Your Sample

A simple finger-prick blood collection in the comfort of your own home. Takes just a few minutes. Pop your sample into the prepaid envelope and send it to our partner laboratory for analysis.

3

Receive Your Personalised Report

Within 7–10 days, your detailed wellness report lands in the Arq app. Understand your key biomarkers across mental clarity, emotional balance, physical vitality, and energy levels — all presented in a clear, beautiful format.

4

Your Tailored Wellness Plan

Based on your results, we curate a personalised nutrition plan and product recommendations from our collection. Your tailored Arq product box is then assembled and delivered to your door.

5

Track, Learn & Evolve

Use the Arq app to track your progress over time, log how you feel, and discover new products suited to your evolving needs. Quarterly reassessments keep your plan aligned with your body as it changes.

About

A sanctuary for the soul, devoted to nurturing the realms of mind, body, and spirit as one.

We seek out objects and practices to cultivate inner peace and alignment, inspired by the beauty of the natural world and the wisdom of ancient traditions. Every product in our collection is chosen with intention — to support your journey toward wholeness.

Our Vision

Arq was born from the belief that true well-being is not a destination but a daily practice — one that honours the connection between our inner landscape and our outer world. We bridge modern science with timeless rituals, offering personalised guidance through our blood-sampling subscription alongside a curated collection of products that nourish every dimension of your being.
